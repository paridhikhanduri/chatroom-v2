welcome

in a world where large language models have become a daily assistant to writing our texts, emails, and other exchanges, how does this ai-mediation impact our interpersonal communication?

chat-room is part live action role-playing experience, part real-time chat app, part two-player game, and part social experiment.

how it works:

  1. pair up: connect with a partner on the other side.
  2. role play: enact out scenarios of either an office worker, a friend, or a romantic interest, navigating a specific task to communicate.
  3. see where that leads you & your conversation!
